HOW TO PLAY MANCALA

Mancala is a 2-player game in which players take turns, one by one, to make a move. The board has six small pits on each side and a store at left and right end of the board. At the beginning of the game 4 candies are placed in each pit. The number on top of each pit/store indicates the number of candies in that pit/store. You control the six pits on your side of the board. Your score is the number of candies in your store. The goal of the game is to capture more candies in the store than your opponent.

In your turn, you choose a pit in your control. The chosen pit is emptied. The chosen pit's candies are dropped one by one into subsequent pits and store except for opponent's store, in a counter-clockwise direction, until the candies run out.

The last dropped candy and all the 5 candies in opposite pit are captured and placed in Player 1's store and hence the score increases by 6.

The game ends when all the six pits on either side of the board are empty.
