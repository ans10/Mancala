HOW TO PLAY MANCALA

1. The goal of the game is to capture more candies than your opponent. The board has six small pits on each side and a store at each end. At the beginning of the game an equal number of candies are placed in each pit. The number on top of each pit/store indicates the number of candies in that pit/store.

You control the six pits on your side of the board. Your score is the number of candies in your store.
